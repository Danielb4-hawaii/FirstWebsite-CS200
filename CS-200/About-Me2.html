About Me

Hi all! My name is Daniel Brooks, however, I go by Clay. I have been going by that
name since I was born so as you can imagine I am pretty attached to it. I am from
Chugiak, Alaska which is absolutely my favorite place in the entire world. While in
high school I dabbled in construction work and restaurant/hospitality services. I plan
to become an architect after I finish school. I have always found houses and buildings
so interesting and I love to tear them apart and see every last aspect, so I think the
career choice is pretty reasonable.

This class is not part of my major so I am taking it as an elective, though I can promise
I won't treat it as anything less than a required course! The final project looks challenging
and fun at the same time so I am optimistic about it. Never have I ever designed a website
before, though I did use Google Sites during my high school digital composition class. I am
not sure if this will directly help my career in any way but it seems fun and like a good class
to take so nonetheless I am here. I look forward to this semester with everyone!
